Our Story

MDRestaurant started with a simple question: “Where do we eat today?” Too many choices, not enough clarity. We wanted to create a platform that feels like a local friend who already knows the best spots in town.

Today, we work closely with restaurants, cafés, and dessert bars in Salem to bring you menus, photos, ratings, and offers you can trust – all in one place.

2023 – The idea is born

We began by mapping the most-loved food spots in Salem, area by area.

2024 – Partnering with restaurants

Local restaurants joined us to offer exclusive deals and real-time updates.

Today – Growing with our diners

Thousands of guests use MDRestaurant every month to discover where to eat next.

What we care about

Every feature we build is guided by three principles:

  • Trust: Verified information and ratings.
  • Ease: Simple, clean, and fast experience.
  • Local love: Boosting Salem’s food culture.

Frequently asked questions

Is MDRestaurant available only in Salem?

We are currently focused on Salem and nearby areas so we can offer deep, accurate coverage. As we grow, we’ll expand to more cities.

Do you charge users any fee?

Using MDRestaurant to browse restaurants and offers is free for diners. Some partner restaurants may run paid experiences or premium events.

I own a restaurant. How do I partner with you?

Scroll down to the contact section or write to us at partners@mdrestaurant.com. Our team will get back to you with the next steps.

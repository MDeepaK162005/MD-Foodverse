What we offer

End-to-end support for diners, groups, and restaurant partners.

Restaurant discovery

Browse by area, cuisine, rating & budget.

Explore handpicked restaurants across Salem with photos, menus, and real user ratings to help you decide quickly.

We continuously update listings to reflect new openings, menu changes, and trending spots—so you’re always up to date.

Table reservations

Skip queues at popular places.

Reserve a table at partner restaurants during peak timings and get instant confirmation for your booking.

Special occasions? Tell us your preferences and we’ll add notes for the restaurant so they can prepare something extra.

Exclusive offers

Make every outing pocket-friendly.

Unlock flat discounts, combo deals, and seasonal offers curated with our restaurant partners.

We negotiate with partners so you get transparent, upfront pricing—no surprise charges at the table.

Group & corporate dining

For teams, families & celebrations.

Planning a team lunch, birthday, or family get-together? We help you shortlist venues and coordinate with restaurants.

From set menus to per-head pricing, we’ll help you find options that match your budget and expectations.

Waitlist & reminders

Never miss a table again.

Join a waitlist at busy restaurants and get notified when your table is almost ready.

Reminders help you reach on time, while our coordination with the restaurant keeps your slot safe.

Dedicated support

Human help when you need it.

Our support team is available to assist with reservations, changes, and special requests.

Reach us via chat, email, or phone. We work closely with restaurants to resolve issues quickly and fairly.
